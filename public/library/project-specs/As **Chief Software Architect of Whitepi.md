As **Chief Software Architect of Whitepine**, I lead the design of a groundbreaking civic-tech platform that puts political power directly in citizens’ hands. Whitepine integrates blockchain, AI, mobile, journalism, and crowd-sourced data to give people the ability to influence government with transparency and trust.

I oversee the **system architecture**, defining secure, scalable APIs and infrastructure across mobile, web, and cloud. I created the **cryptographic trust model** where citizens generate private keys, signing all testimony so input is provably authentic and unaltered. A synchronization protocol lets users carry their civic identity across devices safely.

I architected **Political Capital**, a fungible civic token that citizens earn, ration, or purchase within ethical limits, then expend to amplify initiatives, lower reporting thresholds, and demand action. Its controlled tokenomics prevent speculation and hoarding while rewarding active engagement.

I pioneered **Sandpore visualizations**, turning individual “grains” of participation into collective patterns that dynamically shift to reveal public will. This allows citizens and officials to instantly perceive sentiment disparities without complex math.

Key successes include:

* Partnering with San José District 5 to demonstrate citizen-powered policy insights on anti-blight initiatives.
* Building a **modular microservice architecture** with MongoDB Atlas, Mapbox MTS, and blockchain nodes, supporting millions of participants.
* Establishing Whitepine as a **civic feedback loop**, guaranteeing that once public thresholds are reached, politicians must respond with verifiable action.

Whitepine transforms democracy into a living, accountable system by combining technology, transparency, and the aggregated voice of the people.
